🗳️ Released the <a href="https://lszoszk.github.io/hrc-voting/"><strong>HRC Voting dashboard</strong></a> — an interactive dashboard and reproducible dataset covering every roll-call vote of the UN Commission on Human Rights (1946–2006) and the Human Rights Council (2006–present): <strong>6,346 resolutions and 80,159 individual country votes</strong>, harvested from the OHCHR Search Library. The dataset is archived on <a href="https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.21281232">Zenodo (DOI)</a> and the full harvesting pipeline is open source on <a href="https://github.com/lszoszk/hrc-voting">GitHub</a>.
