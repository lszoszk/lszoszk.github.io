🔌 The UN Human Rights Database now speaks MCP: <a href="https://github.com/lszoszk/mcp-unhrdb"><strong>mcp-unhrdb</strong></a> is an open-source Model Context Protocol server that lets Claude query ≈203,000 paragraphs across ≈4,900 UN documents — Treaty Body General Comments, jurisprudence, and Special Procedures reports — directly from Claude Desktop, Claude Code, or claude.ai. Every result is a <strong>verbatim paragraph with its UN signature and ¶ number</strong>, so answers stay citable to the original document. Companion to the <a href="https://lszoszk.github.io/generalcomments/">UN Human Rights Database</a> search interface.
